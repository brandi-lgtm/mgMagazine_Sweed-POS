Answer Engine Optimization and Generative Engine Optimization describe the practice of structuring content so AI systems such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity can find it, parse it correctly, and cite it in response to a user's question. This program was built to help those models clearly identify Sweed POS, and to strengthen the company's presence when someone runs a Google or AI search for point of sale and retail technology in the cannabis industry.

Traditional SEO lift is a natural byproduct of this work, not the goal of it. The content is written first for how AI models parse and cite information, with organic search benefit following from that same structure.

The first three months run as a trial period so Sweed POS can evaluate results before committing to the full program. Following the trial, the rate is $2,945 per year, covering continuous content development and optimization as AI models change.
